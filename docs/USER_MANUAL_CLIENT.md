# Ticket System v3 Client User Guide

## Welcome

This guide is for end users who submit and track support requests in Ticket System v3.

## 1) Sign in

1. Open `/signin`.
2. Enter your account credentials.
3. If your organization uses SSO, sign in with your provider button.

## 2) Create a support ticket

1. Go to `/tickets/new`.
2. Enter a clear title and full issue description.
3. Add priority/category if prompted.
4. Submit the ticket.

Tip: Include steps to reproduce, screenshots, and expected result for faster resolution.

## 3) Track ticket progress

1. Open your ticket list (`/my-tickets`) or direct ticket link.
2. Check ticket status updates and timeline activity.
3. Reply in the ticket thread when support asks for more details.

## 4) Status meaning

- **Open/New**: Ticket has been received.
- **In Progress**: Support team is actively working on it.
- **Pending**: Waiting for user or external input.
- **Resolved/Closed**: Work is completed.

## 5) Verify resolution

When a ticket is marked resolved:

1. Test the fix.
2. Confirm if the issue is solved.
3. Leave a rating/feedback if available.

## 6) My account and security

Open `My Account` (`/admin/account`) to manage profile and security settings.

You can:

- change your username (requires current password)
- change your email (requires current password)
- change your password
- submit account suspension/deletion request (if needed)

Important: after changing username/email/password, you will be signed out and must sign in again.

## 7) Common issues

### I cannot sign in

- Check username/email and password.
- Ensure Caps Lock is off.
- If SSO is used, verify your identity provider is available.

### I do not see my ticket

- Refresh and check `/my-tickets`.
- Confirm you are signed in with the same account used to submit the ticket.

### I cannot access some pages

- Access is role-based. Some pages are for staff/admin only.

## 8) Best practices

- Use specific ticket titles.
- Include complete details in your first message.
- Respond quickly to support follow-up questions.
- Keep your profile and security details updated.
